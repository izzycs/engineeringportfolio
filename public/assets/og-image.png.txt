OG IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

To create your OG image:

1. Take a screenshot of your 3D portfolio in action
2. Recommended size: 1200x630 pixels
3. Save as: public/assets/og-image.png

Alternatively, use a design tool to create a branded image:
- Include: "Izzy Amaya - Data Engineer"
- Show: A preview of the 3D room
- Style: Match your portfolio aesthetic
- Format: PNG or JPG

Tools you can use:
- Take a screenshot with your browser DevTools at 1200x630
- Use Figma, Canva, or Photoshop
- Use a screenshot tool and crop to 1200x630
